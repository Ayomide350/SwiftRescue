SwiftRescue - Text-to-Speech First Aid Guide

Burn Treatment Instructions

Cool the burn with cool(not cold) running water for 20 minutes.
Remove any tight clothing or jewelry near the burn site.
Cover burn with a clean, non-stick dressing.
Do not pop blisers.
Seek medical help for severe or large burns.

Bleeding Control Instructions

Apply pressure with a clean cloth to stop bleeding.
If possible, raise the injured area above the heart.
Do not remove the cloth if it soaks through; add more layers.

Fracture Stabilization Instructions

If a fracture is suspected, avoid moving the injured limb.
Immobilize it with a splint or sling in the position found.
Apply padding around the fracture to reduce movement.
Do not try to realign the bone.
Seek medical help immediately.

Shock Management Instructions

Lay the person down and elevate their legs unless there's an injury that prevents it.
Keep them warm and comfortable.
Loosen tight clothing and reassure them.
Do not give food or drink.
Seek emergency medical attention promptly.

Airway Maintenance Instructions

If the person is unconscious, check for breathing.
Tilt the head back slightly and lift the chin to open the airway.
Remove any visible obstructions from the mouth.
If not breathing, begin CPR and call for emergency help immediately.
